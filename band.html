Our Story

Formed in 2018,Crimson Echo emerged from the vibrant music scene of Seattle with a unique sound that blends alternative rock with melodic undertones. Our journey began in small clubs and has taken us to stages across the country.

With two studio albums and multiple tours under our belt, we continue to evolve our sound while staying true to our roots. Our music is a reflection of life's contrasts - the quiet and the loud, the gentle and the intense.

We believe in the power of music to connect people and create moments of pure emotion. Each performance is a unique experience, and we invite you to be part of our story.
